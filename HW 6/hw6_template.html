Homework 6: Census data choropleth

Kayce Arkinson

The cholorpleth I designed for this homework exercise is projected in the Georgia Statewide Lambert (ESRI: 102604). The map joins data from the 2021 U.S. Census about household income in the last 12 months within each county of Georgia. I used the state government of Georgia's GIS hub to find the county shapefile. Income increases from yellow to pink.
